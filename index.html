Kandi Ki Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g Kandi (Toor Dal)
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • 1/2 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 2-3 Dried Red Chillies
  • A few Curry Leaves

Instructions

  1. Wash the kandi (toor dal) in water and soak it for 30 minutes.
  2. Drain the water and add the soaked dal to a pressure cooker.
  3. Add 2 cups of water, salt, and turmeric powder to the dal in the cooker.
  4. Cook the dal in the pressure cooker for 3 whistles or until it becomes soft and mushy.
  5. Once the dal is cooked, heat ghee in a pan.
  6. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  7. Add dried red chillies and curry leaves to the pan.
  8. Pour the seasoned ingredients over the cooked dal and mix well.
  9. Enjoy your delicious Kandi Ki with steamed rice!
